Our Story

Our Journey to Property Excellence

Now On Site was founded with a vision to revolutionize property maintenance by offering on-demand solutions that prioritize customer satisfaction and property well-being. With years of experience in the industry, our team identified a need for a service provider that could deliver prompt, reliable assistance for all property maintenance needs.

Expertise and Experience

A Tapestry of Expertise and Experience

Our team brings a wealth of experience from leading companies in the industry, including Curry's, John Lewis, British Gas, and partnerships with several housing associations in London and the South East. With a background in appliance installation and repair, electrical work, locksmith services, and more, we have honed our skills to ensure the highest quality service for our clients.
